# Homework Study Index

This index turns the `HW1`, `HW2`, `HW3`, and `HW4` folders, plus the top-level `HW5` files, into a study sequence.
The study guides are:

- `HW1/STUDY_GUIDE.md`
- `HW2/STUDY_GUIDE.md`
- `HW3/STUDY_GUIDE.md`
- `HW4/STUDY_GUIDE.md`
- `HW5_STUDY_GUIDE.md`

## Suggested order

1. `HW1`
   Builds optimization intuition: random search, coordinate search, coordinate descent, and basic `autograd` usage.
2. `HW2`
   Moves to concrete optimization routines and regression fits, including transformed least-squares ideas that reappear in Chapter 10.
3. `HW3`
   Lines up directly with the provided Chapter 6 and Chapter 7 material on two-class and multi-class classification.
4. `HW4`
   Lines up directly with the provided Chapter 7, Chapter 8, and part of Chapter 10 material on mini-batching, autoencoders, PCA, K-means, and nonlinear models.
5. `HW5`
   Mixes one strong Chapter 10 nonlinear-regression exercise with later material on conv nets, kernelization, model tuning, and tree regression that goes beyond the PDFs provided here.

## Important limitation

Only Chapters `6` through `10` were provided explicitly in this workspace.
Because of that:

- the `HW3` and `HW4` guides can cite the textbook very directly
- the `HW1` and `HW2` guides are annotated honestly as prerequisite / warm-up material when their original source chapters fall outside the provided PDFs
- the `HW5` guide cites Chapter 10 directly where possible and marks the Chapter 11-14 style material as beyond the supplied textbook files

## Fastest path if you are reviewing for a test

- Start with `HW3/STUDY_GUIDE.md`
- Then read `HW4/STUDY_GUIDE.md`
- Then read `HW5_STUDY_GUIDE.md` for the Chapter 10 nonlinear-regression piece
- Use `HW2/STUDY_GUIDE.md` for optimization and transformed-regression review
- Use `HW1/STUDY_GUIDE.md` only as a mechanics refresher
